Strong Community Operating for Risk Reductions, Inc operates for the safety of our citizens and clients by offering programs of prevention and assistance for drug related social issues.

Risk Reduction

Mandatory alcohol education, assessment require attendedance at DUI prevention programs geared toward alcohol education or therapy.

By offering "DUi School" or "AEP", alcohol education programs we aim to, reduce, or eliminate harsher penalities in drunk-driving cases, such as court fines, licenses suspension, and jail time. DUI offenders who sucessfully complete the terms of their alcohol educationor therapy program may have their driving privileges restored.

Family Counseling

The court may require parents or any other party involved n a custody or visitation dispute, and the minor child, to participate in outpatient counseling with a licensed mental health professional, or through other community programs and services that provide appropriate counseling, including, but not limited to, mental health or substance abuse services, for not more than one year, provided that the program selected has counseling available for the designated period of time.
